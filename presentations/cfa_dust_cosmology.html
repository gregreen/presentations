Forward model

$$ \require{color} \definecolor{star}{RGB}{200,140,90} \definecolor{model}{RGB}{90,200,200} f_{\lambda}\left(\lambda,{\color{star}\Theta}\right) = {\color{gray} \underbrace{ \color{white} F_{\lambda}\left(\lambda,{\color{star}\Theta};{\color{model}W}\right) }_{\mathrm{neural\ net}} } {\color{star}\varpi^2} e^{-{\color{star}E} {\color{model}R}\left(\lambda\right)} $$

Stellar parameters:

  • $\Theta =$ type (LAMOST)
  • $\varpi =$ parallax (Gaia)
  • $E =$ extinction (Bayestar19)

Model parameters:

  • $W =$ neural net weights
  • $R =$ extinction curve
